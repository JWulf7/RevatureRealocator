What is Revature Realocator ?

Revature Realocator helps Revature employees who are relocating to a new area find a place to live!

Combining the Realtor API with social networking, Revaure Realocator allows employees to:

  • Search for publicly listed rentals
  • Search for Revature employees who have available spaces for rent
  • Search for Revature employees who are looking for spaces to rent
  • View accesibility to rental properties using the Walk Score API

Advance your career, but take it easy at home!
